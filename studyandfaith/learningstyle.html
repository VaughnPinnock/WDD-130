Was it the subject that was just really interesting? or did they find a way to bring across the material that you really enjoyed? The truth is that we all learn differently Visual, Aural, Read/Write and Kinesthetics (VARK) and the sooner we know what learning style is best for us then the better we will be able to do meaningful studying.
