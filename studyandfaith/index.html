Seek ye learning from the best books

If you are like me, you might have often watched general conference and read the Liahona and be in absolute amazement of the leaders of the church leaders. The also seem to be so well read and knowledgeable on wide cross- section of topics. If you have wondered how they got to be that way the answer to that question can be found in the Doctrine & Covenants, Section 88 seek ye out of the best book words of wisdom; seek learning, even by study and also by faith. This is your chance to learn how.

The leaders that we know and love have devoted their lives to keeping this commandment and we can all do it to! We all lead busy lives and it can sometimes seem like a great task to seek learning from the best books however the key to learning by study and faith is consistency. ByStudyandFaith will help you to develop simple but effective habits which will truly help you to feast upon the words of Christ.

Additionally, it will help you to know what those best books of wisdom are and how you can find them. No matter where you are on the covenant path back to Heavenly Father, we can always find ways to improve and refine your methods, welcome to ByStudyandFaith.

“Wisdom is not a product of schooling but of the lifelong attempt to acquire it.” - Albert Einstein -
